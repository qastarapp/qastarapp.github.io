Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We built Qastar so your life stays yours. We don't sell your data, we don't read your journal, and we never will. This page explains exactly what we collect, why, and how.

What Qastar is

Qastar is a private life-capture app for iOS and Android. You capture moments each day, share them with a small circle you choose, and build a personal archive over time. There is no public feed, no algorithmic recommendations, and no advertising — ever.

What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to make the app work.

  • Account information: your name and email address, used to create and identify your account.
  • Moments and content: the photos, text, and mood data you choose to post. These are stored securely on Firebase and are visible only to you and the circle members you explicitly share them with.
  • Letters: private messages you compose and send. These are visible only to the sender and recipient.
  • Circle data: who you have added to your circle and who has added you. This is used only to determine what content is shared with whom.
  • Usage data: basic app usage signals such as streak counts and moment timestamps, stored privately per account.
  • Device tokens: used solely to deliver push notifications for new moments and letters in your circle.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect advertising identifiers
  • We do not build behavioural profiles
  • We do not track your location unless you explicitly attach it to a moment
  • We do not share any data with third-party advertisers
  • We do not sell your data, ever, to anyone

Who can see your content

Private moments are visible only to you.

Circle moments are visible only to the members of your circle — people you have explicitly chosen and invited.

Letters are visible only to the person you addressed them to.

Qastar staff cannot read your moments or letters. We do not access personal content unless legally compelled to do so, and we will notify you if that ever occurs unless prohibited by law.

Firebase and infrastructure

Qastar uses Google Firebase for authentication, data storage, and push notifications. Firebase stores data on Google's infrastructure. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use any other third-party analytics or tracking services in the app.

Data retention

Your content remains in Qastar for as long as your account is active. If you delete a moment, it is removed from our servers. If you delete your account, all associated content — moments, letters, and account data — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any data we hold about you. You can delete your account at any time from within the app. For any questions or requests, contact us at hello@qastar.app.

Children

Qastar is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us at hello@qastar.app.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you through the app. Continued use of Qastar after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: hello@qastar.app
