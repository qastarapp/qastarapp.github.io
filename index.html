Qastar is not a social network.

There is no public feed. No strangers. No algorithm deciding what matters. Just you, the people you choose, and a quiet archive that grows for years.

✦ · ✦

What Qastar is

✦
One moment, each day
A photo, a thought, or both. A writing prompt helps on days you don't know what to say. No pressure to make it perfect. Just honest.
✦
The Orb
A glowing, breathing circle on your home screen that grows subtly as your archive grows. No numbers. No metrics. Just presence, quietly accumulating.
✦
Your Circle
Share moments with up to 20 people you explicitly choose. No one else can ever see them. They react, comment, and share their own life back with you.
✦
Memories & streaks
Every moment builds a private archive. On This Day surfaces who you were. A streak and mood snapshot give you a pattern, not a performance metric.
